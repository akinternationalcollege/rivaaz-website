The Visionary

Dr. Anupam Sharma

Founder, AK International College & The AK Group

The story of Rivaaz begins long before the first line of code was written. It begins with a seed capital and a massive dream in 2004, when Dr. Anupam Sharma laid the foundation for what would become a hospitality empire.

Driven by unstoppable determination and a sharp vision, Dr. Sharma transformed a modest investment into AK International College—a prestigious institution that officially opened its doors in the 2021-22 academic year. He didn't just build a college; he built a standard.

His empathic leadership and innovative teaching methods have shaped the region's approach to service. Rivaaz is built upon this exact DNA: the belief that true hospitality requires steadfastness, grit, and an unwavering commitment to the guest.

"Carving a path for others to follow, exemplifying courage and ambition."
